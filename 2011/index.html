About

PyCon Finland is a non-profit conference organized by a non-profit organization
Python Finland. It's meant for Python hackers and people interested in Python.

PyCon Finland 2011 will be held on October 17-18 in Turku. Admittance fee will be 100/50/10 €
(see registration for details). We'll have talks on the first day, and the second day is
reserved for sprints, workshops and the biannual meeting of Python Finland.

The venue for the conference is ICT Building, located at Joukahaisenkatu 3, 20520 Turku,
Finland. There are many ways to arrive, and we have gathered information on the venue,
travel, etc. There's also a guest list for pre-conf activities.

We're on Twitter, Facebook and Lanyrd! You can also contact us via email.

Registration

We have three ticket categories:

  • 1. Corporate, 100 € for people whose attendance is paid by their employer. The higher fee
    helps us to keep normal and student prices low! Includes coffee, lunch and dinner.
  • 2. Normal, 50 € for people who pay their own attendance fee. Includes coffee, lunch and dinner.
  • 3. Student, 10 € for students. Includes coffee (There are cheap lunches available for
    students at the cafeteria.)

Note that due to the generosity of our great sponsors, the normal ticket also
now includes dinner!

Please note that there's only coffee serving on tuesday, i.e. no lunch or dinner.

Register to the coolest Python event in Finland by using the form below or, if there's plenty
of you (for example, from the same company), toss a mail to hallitus@python.fi and we'll
handle the registration for you!
