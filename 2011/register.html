About

PyCon Finland is a non-profit conference organized by a non-profit organization Python Finland. It's meant for Python hackers and people interested in Python.

In 2011 PyCon Finland will be held 17.-18.10. Admittance fee will be 100/50/10 € (see registration for details). First day will be talks and the second day will be sprints including the bi-annual meeting of Python Finland.

The venue for the conference is ICT Building located at Joukahaisenkatu 3, 205250 Turku, Finland. There are multiple ways to get there and another local conference, Turku Agile Day provides an excellent guide of how to get here!

We're also on Twitter, Facebook and Lanyrd!

Registration

We have three ticket categories:

  • Corporate, 100 € for people whos attendance is paid by their respective company. Higher feed paid by you helps us to keep normal and student prices low! Dinner in the evening is included in the corporate fee.
  • Normal, 50 € for people who pay their own attendance fee. Does not include dinner.
  • Student, 10 € for students. Does not include lunch nor dinner, but there're cheap lunches available at the cafeteria.
