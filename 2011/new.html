About

PyCon Finland is a non-profit conference organized by a non-profit organization Python Finland. It's meant for Python hackers and people interested in Python.

If you'd like to talk about us please use the pyconfi tag on Digg, delicious, Flickr or your blog. #pyconfi for Twitter, naturally. This way we can snoop out what you’re saying, and all your cool Python friends can find us more easily.

Contact us: email, Twitter, Facebook.
