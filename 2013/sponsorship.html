Python Finland is the local user group in Finland and Scandinavia area. Python Finland has about hundred active members. Our organization was founded in 2011 in order to promote Python knowledge, usage and education in Finland. Our main activity is the annual PyCon Finland event which gathers together Python activists for learning, networking and sprinting. PyCon Finland is a part of the international PyCon conference program.

We are contacting you as your organization is using and promoting Python technology and therefore you might be interested in sponsoring the PyCon Finland event. The two day event will take place in Espoo 22. - 23.10.2012. The first day of the event is for presentations and the second day is reserved for sprinting of Python related topics. In the evening of the first day there will be a dinner event for the participants.
