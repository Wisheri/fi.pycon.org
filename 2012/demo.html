ABOUT

PYCON FINLAND IS A NON-PROFIT CONFERENCE ORGANIZED BY A NON-PROFIT ORGANIZATION PYTHON FINLAND. IT'S MEANT FOR PYTHON HACKERS AND PEOPLE INTERESTED IN PYTHON.

PYCON FINLAND 2011 WILL BE HELD ON OCTOBER 17-18 IN TURKU. ADMITTANCE FEE WILL BE 100/50/10 € (SEE REGISTRATION FOR DETAILS). WE'LL HAVE TALKS ON THE FIRST DAY, AND THE SECOND DAY IS RESERVED FOR SPRINTS, WORKSHOPS AND THE BIANNUAL MEETING OF PYTHON FINLAND.

THE VENUE FOR THE CONFERENCE IS ICT BUILDING, LOCATED AT JOUKAHAISENKATU 3, 20520 TURKU, FINLAND. THERE ARE MANY WAYS TO ARRIVE, AND WE HAVE GATHERED INFORMATION ON THE VENUE, TRAVEL, ETC. THERE'S ALSO A GUEST LIST FOR PRE-CONF ACTIVITIES.

WE'RE ON TWITTER, FACEBOOK AND LANYRD! YOU CAN ALSO CONTACT US VIA EMAIL.
